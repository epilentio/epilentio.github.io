For Digital Nomads

Our modern rental apartments in Croatia's tranquil Split region are a digital nomad's dream. With scenic views, a serene mountainside location, and beaches nearby our apartments offer the perfect blend of productivity and relaxation. We offer you the fastest and most reliable internet connection provided by Starlink. Benefit from off-season rates for an affordable and peaceful remote work experience.

Our Apartments

Discover the perfect getaway at Epilentio's modern rental apartments, known as Lambda Apartments. Situated in the beauty of Croatia's Split region. Located in a peaceful and tranquil mountainside location surrounded by lush olive groves, our apartments provide the ideal escape.

Location

The beach is just a short 250m stroll away (approximately 15 minutes on foot or a quick 2-minute drive), and the vibrant cities of Split and Omis are reachable within 15 minutes by car.

Amenities

With a total of four apartments, including two inviting two-room apartments and two spacious three-room apartments, we offer a variety of choices to suit your needs. Each apartment boasts a private entrance, dedicated parking space, and breathtaking views of the Adriatic Sea and the picturesque island of Brac. Inside, you'll find well-appointed spaces with a bedroom, living room, kitchen, and a convenient bathroom – ideal for solo travelers, couples, or families of four. Plus, stay connected effortlessly with top-notch internet connectivity provided by Starlink.
