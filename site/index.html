Technologies

At Epilentio, we leverage the strength of functional programming languages like OCaml or Haskell to engineer robust and high-quality software. These languages enable us to eliminate the vast majority of bugs by design, thanks to features like a strong type system and immutability.
This approach not only results in more reliable software but also significantly boosts development speed by reducing debugging time and simplifies long-term maintenance.
With Nix/NixOS, we ensure deterministic and reproducible builds, enabling reliable deployments and system configuration management, and ultimately allowing us to provide zero-downtime operations for our clients.
